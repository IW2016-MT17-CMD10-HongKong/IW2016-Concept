怒叱

Live longer with Nùchì

One fifth of china’s soil contaminated

China’s air and water pollution is more visible than its soil pollution. But recent studies have shown heavy-metal pollution tainting china’s africultural lands and its food supply. As a result, many farms use more chemicals for their crops.

Nùchì

Hong Kong imports more then 90% of its food. Additives and preservatives are added to the imported food to preserve it longer and retain its taste. Because of these substances the risk of food poisoning and getting sick is greater. The government investigates 216 to 350 cases of food poisoning each year. In each case 924 to 1360 people were poisoned (68%). Because additives and the big quantity of food poisonings the risk of bad substances in the body of the Hong Kong people is greater. Therefor we build a smart chip that checks the different bad substances through your sweat. Our smartphone app will give you some eating advice that will be based on the collected data of the chip. Hereby we are trying to help the Hong Kong people to minimize the risk of getting sick and increase their immune system.
